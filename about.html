Our Story

Founded in 1995, Success PVT Secondary School began with a simple vision: to provide quality education that nurtures both academic excellence and character development. What started as a small school with just 45 students has grown into one of Malawi's most respected secondary institutions.

Located in Chichiri, Blantyre, our school has consistently maintained high standards of education, producing students who excel in national examinations and go on to become leaders in various fields. Our commitment to holistic education ensures that every student receives not just academic knowledge, but also the skills and values needed to succeed in life.

Over the past three decades, we have expanded our facilities, added modern laboratories, a well-stocked library, computer labs, and sports grounds. Today, we serve over 500 students with a team of 25 dedicated teachers who are passionate about shaping the next generation of leaders.

"Building futures through quality education and character development."

Our Mission

To provide quality, inclusive, and holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, critical thinking, and moral integrity, preparing students to become responsible and productive citizens.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded, innovative, and ethical leaders who positively impact their communities and the nation at large.
