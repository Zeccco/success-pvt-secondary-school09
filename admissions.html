Welcome to Success PVT Secondary School

We are delighted that you are considering Success PVT Secondary School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and transparent, ensuring that every student who joins our community finds the right path to success.

Application Steps

Follow these simple steps to apply for admission

1

Complete Application

Fill out the online application form or collect a physical form from our school office.

2

Submit Documents

Submit all required documents including previous school reports and birth certificate.

3

Entrance Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance assessment in core subjects.

4

Admission Decision

Receive admission decision and complete enrollment if accepted.

Admission Requirements

Documents and requirements for admission

Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original & copy)
  • 2 passport-sized photos
  • Previous school reports (last 2 years)
  • Primary School Leaving Certificate (Form 1 applicants)
  • Transfer letter from previous school
  • Immunization records

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about admissions

When does the admissions process begin?

Applications for the 2026 academic year open on September 1, 2025. We encourage parents to apply early as spaces are limited.

Is there an entrance examination?

Yes, students applying for Form 1 are required to take an entrance assessment in Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge. Transfer students may be assessed based on previous school performance.

What are the payment options for fees?

Fees can be paid termly at the school bursar's office or via bank transfer to our school account. Payment plans may be available upon request.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Applications for financial assistance should be submitted along with the admission application.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Absolutely! We encourage parents to schedule a tour of our facilities. Please contact our admissions office to arrange a visit.
