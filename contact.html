Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What are the school hours?

School hours are Monday to Friday from 7:30 AM to 2:30 PM. The office is open from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

How do I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our Admissions page or visit the school office to collect an application form.

Can I schedule a school tour?

Yes! Please contact our admissions office to schedule a tour during school hours.

How do I pay school fees?

Fees can be paid at the school bursar's office or via bank transfer. Contact our bursar for bank details.

What documents do I need for application?

You'll need a completed application form, birth certificate, previous school reports, and 2 passport photos.
